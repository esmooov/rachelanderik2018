Travel

If you are traveling to Albuquerque from New York City, the best option (and a direct flight) is to fly JetBlue from JFK. Right now, the fares are very affordable, so we highly recommend booking your flights soon.

For all travelers: Please be sure to fly into the Albuquerque International Sunport.
